How It Works

Our intelligent system uses a multi-layered approach to optimize traffic flow in real-time.

1

Data Collection

Traffic sensors, cameras, and IoT devices collect real-time data on vehicle counts, speeds, queue lengths, and traffic density across the road network. This data provides a comprehensive view of current traffic conditions.

2

AI Analysis

Our machine learning models process the collected data to understand traffic patterns, identify congestion points, and predict how traffic will evolve in the next 5-30 minutes. The system considers historical patterns, current conditions, and external factors like weather and events.

3

Optimization Algorithm

The core optimization engine calculates the optimal traffic light timings for each intersection based on current and predicted traffic conditions. The algorithm balances wait times across all approaches and prioritizes directions with heavier traffic.

4

Real-time Adjustment

Traffic light controllers receive and implement the optimized timing plans. The system continuously monitors traffic and makes adjustments in real-time to adapt to changing conditions, ensuring optimal flow is maintained.

5

Network Coordination

Adjacent intersections are coordinated to create "green waves" that allow vehicles to move through multiple intersections without stopping, further reducing congestion and improving traffic efficiency across the entire network.
